Scholarships

International Education Grant named after Heydar Aliyev

Starting from the 2024-2025 academic year, the Ministry of Science and Education of Azerbaijan will introduce the "International Education Grant named after Heydar Aliyev." This grant honors Heydar Aliyev's significant contributions to Azerbaijan's place in international relations and supports the education of international students in Azerbaijani higher education institutions.

For more information

Khazar University International Scholarship Program (KUISP)

Khazar University offers merit-based scholarships that do not require a separate application; eligible students are notified in their admission offer. Scholarships are annually reviewed by an academic panel and may be extended based on the student's academic performance.

For more information

ADA University Scholarship Options

At ADA University, students benefit from a variety of financial aid resources designed to support their education and career goals. The university rewards academic merit and performance through scholarships, fellowships, and awards, emphasizing integrity, ethics, and a commitment to societal impact.

For more information

Western Caspian University: Full and Merit-Based Scholarships

Western Caspian University offers full and merit-based scholarships for international students pursuing bachelor's, master's, and doctoral degrees. These scholarships aim to support academically excellent candidates from low-income countries, providing quality education in English at a leading private university in Azerbaijan.

For more information

Intergovernmental Scholarship Program (Hökumətlərarası Təqaüd Proqramları)

he Azerbaijani government offers scholarship programs to promote international education, allowing Azerbaijani students to study abroad and international students to study in Azerbaijan. These scholarships cover tuition, accommodation, and living expenses for undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs, fostering academic collaboration with countries like Hungary, Russia, and China.

For more information
